Hello

I am a college student who is very eager to learn more about programming and computer science. My interest in computer science developed at a young age because computers were and continue to be an integral part of my life. I would spend a significant amount of time with computers using them as my primary source for entertainment and learning. In due time, I grew fascinated with pursuing a field in computer science. Constant learning is an important part of programming and recently I had an experience that reinvigorated my desire for learning. From October 2021 to February 2022 I’ve had the opportunity to participate in the NASA Community College Aerospace Scholars program (NCAS). This program consists of a five-week introductory course into NASA’s scientific research and engineering process. This course prepared me and other participants for an extensive group project where we had the task to design a rover and plan a mission to the moon. NCAS was a completely new experience for me as this was one of my first group projects outside of school involving an extensive amount of dedication and learning. It was also the first time I had to design a project tailored to space exploration and it forced me to change my thinking to solve the unique problems my group faced. From this experience my idea of problem-solving had changed. Instead of the straightforward approach many school assignments require, a professional setting requires a certain level of creativity to overcome engineering challenges.
